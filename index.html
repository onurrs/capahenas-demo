Balloon Tours

Featured Experiences

Experience Cappadocia's breathtaking landscapes from above with our premium balloon adventures.

Standard
Flight
Capahenas Standard Flights offer a simple yet unforgettable way to witness the breathtaking beauty of Cappadocia from above. Perfect for travelers who want to focus on the magical scenery without the added frills, these flights provide around 1 hour in the air—just the right amount of time to soak in the fairy chimneys, sweeping valleys, and surreal landscapes. Operated with 32-person baskets, Capahenas ensures a safe, comfortable, and budget-friendly experience that doesn’t compromise on wonder.

Comfort
Flight
Capahenas Comfort Flights offer a minimum of 1 hour in the air—plenty of time to take in the stunning valleys and unique formations of Cappadocia. These flights are conducted in spacious 28-person baskets, ensuring a more comfortable and enjoyable experience with a limited number of guests. For enhanced performance and stability, we use 500,000 cuft balloons, giving the flight greater lift and control. Capahenas Comfort Flights operate year-round, subject to weather conditions and availability, providing a smooth and memorable journey through the skies.

Private
Flight
Capahenas Private Flight offers a more intimate and premium way to experience the beauty of Cappadocia from above. Operated with a specially designed 24-person basket, the flight is limited to a maximum of 20 guests to ensure extra space and comfort throughout the journey. With approximately 60 minutes in the air, you’ll have plenty of time to soak in the region’s stunning valleys and rock formations in a relaxed setting. Ideal for those who value comfort, personal space, and a more exclusive atmosphere, Capahenas Private Flights deliver a refined ballooning experience.
