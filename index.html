Cappadocia is an ancient district in east-central Anatolia... boundaries have changed over time. Cappadocia's landscape is made up of dramatic areas of soft volcano rock that have been shaped by erosion into towers and cones, valleys and caves.

Amazing topography is matched only by the fascinating human history. The region's soft stones have been used by people for centuries to find shelter underground, leaving behind stunning cavern architecture.

As Capahenas Travel, we will be delighted to help you plan every step of your travel to Cappadocia including flight tickets, airport transfers, hotel accommodation, tours and much more.
