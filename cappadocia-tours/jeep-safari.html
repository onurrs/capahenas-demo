Morning Safari Tour:

The 3-hour Morning Jeep Safari starts at 10:00 am and ends around 1:00 pm. We will pick you up from your hotel in Goreme or Urgup. After that, we'll drive from Goreme to begin our excursion. We will then drop you at your hotel at 13.00.

The trip covers 50 km and takes approximately 3 hours. The tour price includes driver, petrol and taxes.

The driver is a local who speaks limited English. He will take you from one place to another on the site. He will not be able to provide much information about the location. Each Jeep can carry up to five people.

Sunset Safari Tour:

Sunset Safari starts at 16:00 in afternoon. It lasts approximately three hours. We will pick you up at your accommodation in Goreme or Urgup and drive to Goreme. After sunset, we'll pick you up at your hotel and offer wine.

This journey takes approximately 50 km and takes about three hours. Prices include the driver, gasoline and taxes.

He will take you from one site to the next with limited English. He will not be able to describe the sights. Each vehicle is capable of carrying up to five people.

Sunset Jeep Safari rates are 40 Euro per head.

Please send us an email from info@capahenastravel.com to get further information and make your booking.
